Our Mission

At Waterloo Counseling Center (WCC), we endeavor to strengthen the mental health and well-being of Central Texas individuals, couples and families through effective and affordable counseling with an expertise in gender, sexual and cultural diversity. Waterloo - the resource for your journey.
